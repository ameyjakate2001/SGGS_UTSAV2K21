About Us

  "Utsav" is an official cultural club of the Shri Guru Gobind Singhji Institute of Engineering and Technology, Vishnupri, Nanded. Students can show their hidden talent and explore too many new things. It is organised with a view to stimulate the participant's creativity and enable them to gain experience. The cultural festival consists of events with a whole range of activities ranging from quizzes, games, media events, and many more.
